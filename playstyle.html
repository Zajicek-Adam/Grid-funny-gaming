Lane

Lane is the worst phase ever since the nerfs after 2019 Worlds. You have a terrible waveclear, short range, and not much damage. The only time you can threaten a 2v2 kill is with an engage support who can stack your passive. You need to get a good cs amount, otherwise you will be completely useless, however, you likely cannot walk up due to your terrible range.
